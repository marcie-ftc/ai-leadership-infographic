Essential AI Platform Categories

Your AI Leadership Arsenal

1. Salesforce Einstein AI

Paramount for your background. Master the native AI capabilities within the ecosystem you already command.

2. Generative AI Tools

Proficiency in ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude for content, analysis, and communication is non-negotiable for modern leaders.

3. RevOps Platforms

Understand AI in Gong, Clari, and Outreach to grasp sales conversation intelligence and forecasting.

4. Customer Service AI

Familiarize with AI in Zendesk, Intercom, and Drift for ticketing, chatbots, and agent assistance.

5. Business Intelligence (BI)

Leverage AI features within Tableau and Power BI for natural language querying and deeper data insights.

6. Cloud AI Platforms

Grasp the business purpose of Google Vertex AI, Azure AI, and AWS SageMaker to understand deployment potential.
